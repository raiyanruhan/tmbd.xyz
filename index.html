Our Story

How we're changing the digital landscape for women entrepreneurs

01

The Beginning

Founded in 2020, Target Marketing BD started with a simple mission: to provide halal digital marketing solutions for women entrepreneurs who wanted to grow their businesses online while maintaining Islamic values.

02

The Challenge

We noticed many talented women entrepreneurs struggling with digital marketing - either because of limited technical knowledge or concerns about maintaining Islamic principles in their online presence.

03

Our Solution

We assembled a team of female digital marketing experts who understand both the technical aspects and the cultural/religious considerations to create truly halal marketing solutions.
